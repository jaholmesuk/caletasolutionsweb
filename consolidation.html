IT Consolidation

Consolidate and Save Money!

The majority of businesses do not realise IT equipment and services is almost certainly running in their environment which is doing nothing other than costing money in electricity and expensive licensing. IT departments tend to leave legacy servers or networks online without full understanding on cost or security concerns this can cause.

Caleta IT will undertake a full IT audit and site survey that includes gathering your server performance statistics such as CPU , memory , disk and network connectivity and consumption. Once the Audit and survey is complete we will create a full assessment and plan along with our consolidation recommendations. We will go through our recommendations as well as other alternatives which may involve migrating some services into the cloud. Doing so could save your company many thousands in ongoing costs.

We guarantee any recommendations Caleta IT make will save you money. We are so confident our consultants are able to find consolidation recommendations we can offer a free audit and assessment. If we find no recommendations there will be zero charge! Get in contact for a free assessment.
