Our Mission

Our mission is provide sustainable IT Solutions for all businesses.

Not only can we provide a fully managed solution to migrate your critical IT, we can also provide skilled engineers on the ground professionally trained to plan and carry out critical IT migrations. Moving systems to the cloud is an excellent way to reduce your overall carbon footprint.

We believe this sets us apart from all other IT relocation businesses. Hardly any other competitors can offer experienced engineers on the ground whenever a migration occurs. Having skilled engineers available with migration experience on hand if any problems arise massively reduces the risk of a unsuccessful migration. This is especially important if your in-house IT team have no previous IT relocation experience.

On top of this Caleta IT have a vast array of cloud experience and can provide cost saving initiatives to any migration project, as well as recommend other cloud based migration strategies to further reduce risk and cost to your business.
