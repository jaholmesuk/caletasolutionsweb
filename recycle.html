IT Recycling

Let us take care of your E-Waste

Here at Caleta IT we take IT disposal and recycling very seriously, making sure all data is securely wiped to industry standard and taking this complex and time-consuming task off your hands. We will ensure that your disposal meets organisational requirements as well as your legal obligations. Once any data is securely wiped Calata IT can provide certificates of destruction where required. If necessary we can physically destroy the data and physical devices if your IT policies prohibit recycling of equipment.

As part of the solution, Caleta IT will conduct an initial survey to establish whether the equipment holds any resale value. If applicable the value can be offset against the overall recycling costs, and in some cases making the project to recycle the equipment cost neutral.
